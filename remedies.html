Herbal Remedies

Scientically proven remedies you can grow in your garden

  • Aloe Vera
  • Garlic
  • Tea Tree

There is a small amount of evidence that garlic can help with colds, lowering cholesterol, and high blood pressure. Garlic can increase the risk of bleeding so it shouldn't be taken with blood thinners. It can also cause heartburn and upset stomach.

Tea tree has been proven with a small amount of research to aid in the healing of acne, nail fungus, and athlete's foot. Some people have had allergic skin reactions to it. Do not eat it - it can cause severe confusion and loss of muscle coordination.

Aloe vera has a laxative effect when consumed and can reduce blood sugar. There is some evidence to show that it heals psoriasis and rashes.
